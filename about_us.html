  • IMPORTANCE OF TEERTHA SHRADDHA

    According ancient 'vedas and puranas', 'teertha shraddha' performed at kashi,gaya and prayagraj is reffered to as a very auspicious ritual.
    It is also mentioned that every individual is born with three debts (ऋण).Debts from father, God and Sage(rishi).
    By performing 'teertha shraddha' at prayagraj along with 'veni-dana'(married women offering a small length of hair cutting-off from head and immerse IT in sangam. Performed for her soubhagya(long-life of her husband.)) Which is performed only in prayagraj.Helps in freeing one-self from depts from pitru(father)
    Similarly, performing the same at 'gaya' and 'kashi' helps one-self free from their debts from 'deva'(God) and 'rishi'(sage).
