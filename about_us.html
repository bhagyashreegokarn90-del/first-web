ABOUT-US

Our family is rooted to gokarna, karnataka.
Our ancestors came to kashi and served pilgrims and we still carry the legacy.
We provide home-made bramhin food(satvik bhojan) and arrange their stay.

"kashi-yatra" is a sacred duty of every sanatani.
Pilgrims come to kashi with lots of asthetic values to perform various customs.
Many pilgrims with karnataka people in majority come to us to full-fill their various sacred rituals like sharaddha,poojasi>, Asthi visarjan.
We serve them with lots of DEVOTION.
