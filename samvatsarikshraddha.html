SAMVATSARIK SHRADDHA

  • Samvatsarik Shraddha is a significant ritual performed annually to honor and remember deceased ancestors. It is believed that performing this ritual brings peace to the souls of the departed and blessings to the living. The ritual is typically performed by the eldest son or a close relative of the deceased, and it involves offerings of food, water, and prayers to ensure the well-being of the ancestors in the afterlife.
