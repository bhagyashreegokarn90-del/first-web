RUDRA ABHISHEK

Rudrabhishek is a Hindu ritual where Lord Shiva is worshipped in his Rudra form through an abhishek (sacred bath) of the Shivling, accompanied by the chanting of Vedic mantras. It is a powerful puja, which is believed to bestow peace, prosperity, and the removal of obstacles in life. The ritual is often performed during auspicious occasions or to seek blessings for health and well-being.
